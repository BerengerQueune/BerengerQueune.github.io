ManoMano: raise customer's engagement



During a 3 days hackaton. A competition gathered 24 Data teams to analyse a Database given by ManoMano in order to find customer's pain points while purchasing on the website and make suggestion to improve customer's experience.

We analyzed the Database with Power BI then I used a Python library called Text2Emotion in order to identify customer's feelings based on their comments. I also used photoshop to design some of the visuals used in our presentation. We went to the finals where I made an oral presentation of our work to hundreds of alumnis and Data professionals from ManoMano. Our team finished on the podium at the third place. The final presentation is available Here. You need to enter personal information to get access and it starts at 01:25:45. I am the one talking. ManoMano wrote an article on this topic.
