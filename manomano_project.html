ManoMano: raise customer's engagement



Tech stack: Power BI, Python, Sentiment Analysis, Photoshop

During a 3 days hackaton. A competition gathered 24 Data teams to analyse a Database given by ManoMano in order to find their customer's pain points while purchasing on the website. We also had to make suggestion to improve their customer's experience.

We analyzed the Database with Power BI then I used a Python library called Text2Emotion in order to identify customer's feelings based on their comments. I also used photoshop to design some of the visuals used in our presentation. We went to the finals where I made an oral presentation of our work to hundreds of alumnis and Data professionals from ManoMano. Our team finished on the podium at the third place.
