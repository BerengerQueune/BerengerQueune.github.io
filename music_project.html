Determine music track popularity



Link to Github repository
Tech stack: Python, Streamlit, RandomForest Classifier

As a team of 5, during a 2 days hackaton, using a Spotify Database, we developped a webapp for professionals of the music industry. We were free to find our own concept.

After a team brainstorming we picked my concept of modifying music metrics such as danceability, genre, liveness and many others to predict if the track was going to be popular. Music productors could use and experiment with our app to find out what works best for them. My work consisted of using Python to build the Machine Learning Model and integrate it within Streamlit. After many trials, I found the RandomForest Classifier model to be the most efficient. Our Streamlit app is here. I made an oral presentation of our project to hundreds of alumnis. This hackaton was a competition between 24 Data teams and our project reached the finals. After we published our app on Linkedin, we were contacted by a Streamlit employee who congratulated us, sent us goodies and highlighted our work on their own website.
